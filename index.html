🎯 Objects of the Consortium

  • Create market chains within Uganda & global markets
  • Employment for youth, women, men & disabled
  • Help Uganda achieve middle-class status
  • Share knowledge and increase high-quality production
  • Explore new market niches & fair-price raw materials
  • United research centers for premium products
  • Unified marketing platform for easy & gainful sales
  • Strong body to protect & expand member groups
  • Capacity building for supply chain integration
  • Market research in EAC, SADC, ECOWAS & beyond

📍 Operational footprint

Kasese District: Kisinga, Nyakatonzi, Munkunyu sub-counties. Headquarters at MENODA (Mesopotamia Notre Dame Rural Development Association).

Registered under Uganda’s PMA & AVCP — delivering sustainable transformation across diverse agro-ecological zones.
