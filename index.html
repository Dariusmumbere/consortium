Who We Are

United by land,
driven by purpose

Basongora-Bakonzo United Farmers Consortium (BBUFC) is a registered company limited by guarantee, bringing together 20 farmer groups across Kasese District to build sustainable agricultural value chains.

Founded in 2019, we operate under Uganda's Plan for Modernization of Agriculture (PMA), headquartered at MENODA in Mesopotamia — serving communities in Kisinga, Nyakatonzi and Munkunyu sub counties.

Our Mission

What we're
building together

  • 01Create sustainable market chains within Uganda and internationally for member products
  • 02Generate employment for youth, women, men and people with disabilities
  • 03Support Uganda's goal of becoming a middle-income economy through agriculture
  • 04Share knowledge and skills to increase production of quality goods for the East African Common Market
  • 05Form a united marketing platform so members can sell products and services easily and profitably
  • 06Conduct market research in EAC, SADC, ECOWAS and beyond to stay globally competitive
