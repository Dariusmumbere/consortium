Who We Are

Farming that builds communities

BBUFC is a consortium of 20 registered farmer groups organized across 12 agricultural value chains, operating in Kasese District under Uganda's Plan for Modernization of Agriculture (PMA).

Formerly known as Farmers Farming Together For Sustainable Value Chains, we unite producers, processors, and traders to create sustainable market chains within Uganda and across East Africa.

Our work spans food production, agro-processing, soap making, carpentry, and environmental sustainability — all grounded in shared knowledge and mutual growth.

Our Mission

What We're Working Toward

01Create sustainable market chains in Uganda and internationally for our members' products
02Generate employment for youth, women, men and people with disabilities
03Share knowledge and skills to produce high-quality goods for the East African Common Market
04Build a united marketing platform enabling members to sell profitably and easily
05Conduct market research across COMESA, SADC and ECOWAS regions
06Strengthen member capacity to become reliable links in our supply chain
