About This Chain

From seedling to export —
organic cocoa & coffee

The Cocoa and Coffee Production Value Chain is led by the Margharita Organic Vanilla & Coffee Farmers COOP Society, which brings together over 300 farmers across Kasese District. The chain is built on three tiers: seedling producers, farmers, and the cooperative society that organises collection, processing and market access.

Fifteen dedicated cocoa and coffee seedling producers supply the chain's foundation — ensuring consistent, quality planting material for the 300 registered farmers. The COOP Society coordinates dry processing, packaging, and value addition to prepare produce for domestic and export buyers.

Key investments in processing machinery, packaging equipment, and UNBS certification will unlock premium market access and significantly improve income for all chain participants.
